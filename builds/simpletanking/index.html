Arch Angel's Simple Tanking Guide

For race Argonian is really good for resource management (drinking a potion gives an additional 4k resources). Nord is best in slot at the moment giving health, resistances and ultimate regeneration. Imperial in my opinion is the worst race at the moment but it gives you resources and health every few seconds when attacking.

For a simple gear setup to be extremely tanky you can go with Plague Doctor, Fortified Brass and Blood Spawn. If you're fresh out of CP 160 you can go with Plague Doctor (Purchasable) and Whitestrake's Retribution (Craftable).

For the glyphs and traits you'll want to be looking for is to go health glyphs on everything. 6 Body pieces sturdy and then 1 piece reinforced (Chest/Head/legs). For jewellery go for 3 magicka recovery.

With the weapon enchant changes with Wrathstone the best options is to go decisive with a absorb stamina glyph and then on the back bar a ice/lightning staff infused with a crusher enchant (use elemental blockade to proc this). But using a destruction staff is a little more for experienced tanks so if you don't feel comfortable then you can go for infused crusher front bar and infused weapon weakening back bar one handed.

Then for a simple explanation on how to tank: First off is when going into a fight try to Inner Fire enemies at the back or HIGH priority enemies first then switch to your front bar quick to taunt the front enemies, when you've done this Choking Talons the enemies that are on top of you, at this point you can start looking around for enemies to chain pull to you and try to keep Choking Talons the enemy so that they won't move for the DPS.

For boss fights always turn the boss to the opposite side from where you are coming from (opposite of your allies) and never move from that spot.

Pierce Armor
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Enemy
Range: 5 meters
Cost: 1701 Stamina
Skill description
Thrust your weapon with disciplined precision to deal 660 Physical Damage and taunting them to attack you for 15 seconds. Also afflicts enemy with Major Fracture and Major Breach, reducing Physical Resistance and Spell Resistance by 5280 for 12 seconds.

Unrelenting Grip
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Enemy
Range: 22 meters
Cost: 3511 Magicka
Skill description
Launch a fiery chain to grasp and pull an enemy to you, dealing 414 Flame Damage. Also grants you Major Expedition, increasing your Movement Speed by 30% for 4 seconds. If the target cannot be pulled, you restore 100% of the ability's cost as Magicka. This attack cannot be dodged or reflected.

Fragmented Shield
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Self and Allies (6s)
Range: 12 meter radius
Cost: 4050 Magicka
Skill description
Call the earth to your defense, creating a damage shield for you and nearby allies that absorbs 877 damage. You also gain Major Mending, increasing your Healing Done by 25% for 4.5 seconds.

Green Dragon Blood
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Self (23s)
Cost: 4320 Magicka
Skill description
Draw on your draconic blood to heal for 33% of your missing Health. You also gain Major Fortitude and Major Endurance and Minor Vitality, increasing Health Recovery and Stamina Recovery by 20% and healing received by 8% for 20 seconds.

Choking Talons
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Area
Range: 6 meter radius
Cost: 4050 Magicka
Skill description
Call forth talons from the ground, dealing 477 Magic Damage to enemies near you and immobilizing then for 4 seconds. Enemies hit are afflicted with Minor Maim, reducing their damage done by 15% for 7 seconds. An ally near the talons can activate the Ignite synergy, dealing 994 Magic Damage to all enemies held within them.

Target: Area
Range: 20 meter radius
Cost: 250 Ultimate
Skill description

Heroic Slash
Cast Time: Instant
Target: Enemy
Range: 5 meters
Cost: 2970 Stamina

Surprise enemy with a deep lunge, dealing 722 Physical Damage and reducing their Movement Speed by 60% for 12 seconds. Also afflicts enemy with Minor Maim, reducing their damage done by 15% for 12 seconds. You gain Minor Heroism, granting you 1 Ultimate every 1.5 seconds for 9 seconds.

Target: Self

An ally near you can activate the Bone Wall synergy, granting the ally and up to 3 other friendly targets near them a damage shield that absorbs 50% of their Max Health for 6 seconds and Major Vitality increasing their healing received by 30%
