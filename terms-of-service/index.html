Terms of Service

Laws and Rules

General Rules

  • Keep it SFW.
  • Stay nice and respectful to each other.
  • No spamming.
  • No drama.
  • Speak and type in English in all guild locations. (Only exception is 1-on-1 in the private category.)

People that don't uphold to these rules risk being removed from the guild.

Trial Rules

  • Be on time and be prepared (15 min before the trial event is starting).
  • Always listen to the Trial Leaders so they can explain the mechanics and what each role needs to do.
  • Sharing/trading Loot will be done after we finished the event.

How to prepare

  • Make/get yourself Food and Drink buffs.
  • Repair your gear and bring Repair Kits.
  • Have Potions and Soul Gems on you.

People that don't uphold to these rules risk being removed from the trial group and will not be prioritized in future events.

Tip!
If you need any help with your gear or skill setup to optimize it for trials - feel free to ask any officer in the guild.
